Welcome to Cocktail UI

a universal Cocktailmachine control script



1. Create setups


In this section you can create the setups required to stet mixing cocktails with your machine

First you have to push the „create new setup“ button

This will lead you to the input screen where you can name your pumps.
For logic reasons it is recommended to name the pumps after the liquids attached to them.

Furthermore it is here where you enter the ms your machine needs to fill up the required cl.

2. Create recipes based on your setups


First you have to name your Cocktail.
Then you can add information that will show on the screen before you start mixing. For example, you might want to add some advice how to mix correctly or inform about the necessary preparation.
After that you can also add information that users are going to see after mixing the cocktail. For example you might want to tell them to add fruit and decors or ice to your cocktail.
Before you can start mixing, you have to add all the ingredients for your cocktail of course. This you will do by choosing the required pumps (which you hopefully have named after the liquids you attached to them
